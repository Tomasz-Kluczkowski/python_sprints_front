What Do We Do?

Helping Beginners To Get Good

We are open to people who just started to program and need guidance and mentorship. We can help you become a better programmer by collaborating with others and reading loads of code. Come and try it - you will not be disappointed.

Engaging Core Developers

For those of us who are programming on the advanced level there are always new tasks to test their mettle. We fix bugs, we improve implementation and add features. No matter your style you will be able to find a ticket to your liking.
